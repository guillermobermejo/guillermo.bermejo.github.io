My dive into computer science and introduction to programming occurred in 2015 while in pursuit of an associate degree in liberal arts and mathematics. From experience, I knew that mathematics required a high level of critical thinking, critical analysis and problem solving skills in order to be successful, three qualities I exhibit, especially so when going through the process of learning something new. My true passion lies in continuously learning new things be it instruments, concepts, technologies or even languages and am naturally a very hands-on type of guy. This is why in my journey of pursuing an associate degree, I learned that while mathematics satisfied me my true satisfaction came from the process of problem solving itself, I desired a means to apply my skills at a more technical level, which led me to computer science and development.
